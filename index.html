Designing The Engine of
cellular technologies
TurtleTree Scientific was founded in Singapore out of a desire to solve the problems of cellular agriculture. We’re producing food grade growth factors and cell culture media to design the necessary foundations for the industry to thrive. TurtleTree Scientific is started by TurtleTree Labs and is led by an experienced team of industry professionals.

CELL AGRICULTURE

Currently, growth factors and cell culture media account for 55-95% of the marginal cost in cell-based food production. We’re disrupting the costs of these components as a tremendous step forward in cellular agriculture’s progress down the cost curve.

COSMETICS

Growth factors are active ingredients in numerous skin-care products and are essential for healthy skin structure and function. Our ability to produce human-patterned growth factors allows us to tap on this burgeoning segment of beauty and personal care.

PHARMACEUTICAL

There are numerous pharmaceutical applications for these components ranging from their uses in therapeutics to groundbreaking research.
